Instagram Unfollowers Checker

How to download your files from Instagram

  1. Navigate to Profile > Menu (three lines) > Accounts Center > Your information and permissions > Export your information > Create Export
  2. Select which account you want to use the checker on.
  3. Choose where to export.
  4. Before exporting select desired Date Range
    - First time users select "All time."
  5. Start export and wait for it to send to the location you chose
